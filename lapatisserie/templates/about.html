About La Patisserie


La Patisserie is a high-end patisserie shop in Brisbane. We gained reputability by winning an award in 2018 worldwide cake championship in Rome.

More customers in Brisbane are coming to the shop frequently to taste the freshest and finest cakes in the shop. As the customer base has grown significantly in the past two years, We decided to start using e-commerce to sell and deliver cakes to customers in addition to selling cakes in the shop. Customers can search their favourite cakes online and make orders directly from the website.

The delivery is currently only available within the Brisbane area. The delivery fee will be free for purchases above $50.
  • Place your order by 2pm for the next day delivery
  • Free delivery in Brisbane area for purchases more than $50
  • For further enquiries, email us at lapattiserie@cake.com.au

We are excited to serve you better!
